1. Purpose

To identify potential fraud indicators in claims or policy applications.

2. Common Fraud Red Flags

Multiple claims within short period

Claim shortly after policy inception

Inconsistency between narrative and evidence

Claimant not reachable or avoids communication

Repaired parts mismatch damage description

Third-party injury claims without police report

3. Document Tampering Indicators

Altered hospital bills

Fake repair invoices

Forged signatures or missing official stamps

Mismatched fonts or numbering
