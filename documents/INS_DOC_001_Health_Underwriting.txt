Department: Underwriting
Document Type: Eligibility & Risk Assessment SOP
Version: 1.0

1. Purpose

To provide standardized rules and assessment criteria for evaluating applicants applying for individual or family health insurance policies.

2. Applicant Eligibility Criteria

Applicant must be 18 to 65 years (entry age).

Family floater eligible dependents include:

Spouse

Children aged 91 days to 25 years

Senior citizen plans require separate underwriting.

3. Decline / Temporary Hold Conditions

Applicants must be declined or placed on hold under the following conditions:

Active cancer diagnosis

Major organ transplant history

Uncontrolled diabetes (HbA1c > 9%)

HIV positive status

Ongoing hospitalization

Recent major surgeries (< 90 days)

4. Medical Evaluation Requirements

Telemedical screening for all applicants > 45 years

Full medical check-up for:

Smokers above age 40

BMI > 32

Family history of cardiovascular disease

Additional tests may be requested based on declared conditions.

5. Final Decision Rules

Underwriters shall use the following categories:

Standard Accept — no loading, no exclusions

Accept with Loading — increased premium due to risk

Accept with Exclusions — specific conditions not covered

Reject — high-risk profiles based on Section 3
