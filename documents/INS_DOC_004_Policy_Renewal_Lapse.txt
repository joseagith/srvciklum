1. Renewal Eligibility Rules

A policy is eligible for renewal when:

Premium is paid before due date

Grace period of 30 days applicable

Renewals beyond 30 days require reinstatement approval

2. Lapsed Policy Restart Process

Validate reason for lapse

Confirm no claims filed during lapsed period

Customer must:

Submit renewal form

Provide self-declaration of good health

For health policies:

Medical underwriting may be required

On approval:

Issue policy with new start date

Apply waiting periods as per norms

3. Customer Communication

Send SMS + Email reminders 15 days before due date

Notify lapse after 30 days

Provide reinstatement options
