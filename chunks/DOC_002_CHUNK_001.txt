## Full Document

Document ID: INS_DOC_002
Department: Claims
Document Type: End-to-End Workflow SOP
Version: 2.1

1. Claim Registration

Customer calls or submits claim online.

Capture:

Policy number

Vehicle details

Date & time of incident

Location & description

Generate Claim Reference Number.

2. Initial Assessment

Verify policy validity

Confirm coverage type (OD / TP / Comprehensive)

Check prior claims history

Identify potential fraud triggers

3. Surveyor Assignment

Assign external surveyor within 24 hours

Provide:

Claim reference

Contact details

Location of vehicle

Garage information

4. Survey & Damage Estimation

Surveyor responsibilities:

Inspect the vehicle

Capture images

Estimate repair cost

Submit detailed survey report

5. Approval & Repair Process

Compare estimate with policy terms

Approve repair amount

Notify customer & authorized garage

Final bill reconciliation

6. Claim Settlement

Collect final invoice

Verify repairs as per surveyor report

Make payment to garage or customer

Close claim in system
